How to play

  • Pick your player above
  • The object is to flip your opponents discs to your disc color
  • Each player is given 32 discs
  • The player with the most discs on the board at the end of the game wins
  • Disc placement must surround opponents disc in order to flip to your color (your disc must be on either end of your opponents disc)
  • Rows can be horizontal, vertical, and diagonal
